A lightweight, non-custodial layer that builds multi-asset hedged positions on Hyperliquid by executing and maintaining the required legs automatically.

What

We help traders build hedged or relative-value structures by executing the required perp legs on Hyperliquid, bundled into one clean view.

Why

Pure USD exposure amplifies liquidation risk. Correlated legs smooth PnL and reduce blow-ups from one-directional moves.

How

You choose assets and ratios. HyperPairs places and maintains the individual USDC-margined perp positions with correct weights and rebalancing.

Who

Traders expressing ETH vs BTC, SOL vs ETH, sector spreads, beta hedges, or custom multi-asset views without manual multi-leg management.

  • Multi-leg relative-value strategies built on Hyperliquid.
  • Correlation & hedge tools to find positive, negative, or low-correlation combos.
  • Auto-rebalancing & ratio management if legs drift.
  • Executes as separate Hyperliquid perp positions — HyperPairs is just the layer on top.
